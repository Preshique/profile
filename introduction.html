PRECIOUS SOBOWALE-DAVIES

Profile

PRECIOUS SOBOWALE-DAVIES was born on October 27th 1997 in ikeja,lagos Nigeria. The first of four children to the family of Abayomi and kikelomo SOBOWALE-DAVIES. Both Parents are Christians but mrs kikelomo was a muslim before she converted. Precious was born and brought up in lagos all through her life. Precious Father is a business man and her mother works as a civil servant in Ifako-ijaiye. The last born of the family are siamese twins but have been seperated at birth.



Precious completed her Secondary school education at Rose Valley High School, lagos state. Then went on to learn some hand skills like Bead making,Catering and event decoration. After that she got admitted to study Computer Science at The Natonal Open University Of Nigeria in Abeokuta where she's currently in her finals.



Precious is currently also doing a web development program in Ogun Tech Hub in Abeokuta,Ogun State. She's also an online personal shopper for both luxury and non-luxury wears and accessories which she delivers nationwide and worldwide. Her dream is to be a professional coder who is breaking barriers in the tech world.
